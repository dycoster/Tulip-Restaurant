Reservations

The Tulip dining room is open. Please book using the below form.

Bookings can be made by calling 5229 6953, you can contact Tulip via email or make a booking above. If you are unable to find a suitable date/time please call the restaurant, as availability changes daily.

Open hours

Lunch: Fri-Sat from 12pm
Dinner: Tues-Sat from 5.30pm

Functions

To make a group booking for more than 10pp or to enquire about a function please call 5229 6953, or you can email us at info@tuliprestaurant.com.au

Gift Vouchers

Treat a family member, friend or colleague to a meal at Tulip. Vouchers are personalised and valid for three years from the date of purchase.

Please Note: Gift Vouchers are emailed to you, not posted.

Please let us know if you experience any issues receiving your voucher. We are always happy to help. Vouchers are also available for offline purchase, please call us on 5229 6953.
